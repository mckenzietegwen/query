Q1

What do you think of garden gnomes?


...Hm. Ok, since I’m at my parent’s house, I wouldn’t really care because it’s not my garden. If it was my personal place, I’d be like no that’s not going there. Unless there was a really, really good reason.

Tell me a little bit about yourself.


...I feel like since the last time I’ve been in school I’ve done a lot of thinking and reflecting on what I might want to go back to school for; the things I like doing; the things I don’t like doing…and one of the things I enjoy doing is helping people and solving problems.

Both of these questions are intended to loosen a candidate up and see a glimpse into their personality. Accordingly, Anthony's answers equally reveal that he is a problem-solver that thinks very rationally and logically.

Source: This interview question was originally asked in Trader Joe's interviews.
